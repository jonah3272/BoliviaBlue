## What Are Sitelinks?

Sitelinks are the subpages that appear under your main search result in Google, like:
- "Acerca de"
- "More results from bolivianblue.net"

## How to Get Sitelinks

Google automatically generates sitelinks based on:
1. **Site Structure** - Clear navigation hierarchy
2. **Internal Linking** - Important pages linked from homepage
3. **Page Authority** - Pages with good content and traffic
4. **User Behavior** - Pages users click most
